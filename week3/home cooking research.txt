Due to Covid-19, food practices were observed to have changed amongst the Sri Lankan public. Bulk buying and storing of groceries before lockdowns were more common. People used more garlic, ginger, turmeric and asafoetida in their dishes, hoping to fight against infection. Working women said that home cooking gave them the opportunity to bond with their families.
